Tantuvis

For The Weavers

Tantuvi means weavers in Sanskrit the 3500 year old language of ancient India. The works of Hindu philosophy and and compositions were predominantly written in Sanskrit. In Modern India ,the language is now only heard as a recital of prayer or songs in temples but not spoken in India. One can relate weaving as ancient art of India in same level as Sanskrit to ancient India. Fortunately, it still exists and is still striving hard along with the modern textile industry.

Who we are and how are we going to help the Tantuvis? We bring genuine handloom sarees from weavers to you. Our objective is to help the Tantuvis to showcase their products to the world. 'WE-AVE REVIVE... WE SURVIVE' is the voice of many weavers which we are getting it across to you.

It is time that we as global community take action to make this industry survive and encourage the Tantuvis to not stop but weave more. Feel the weave and make them live.
